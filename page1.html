Aetheria

AmazingWow Studios RFP No: 231107-1

Description

Embark on a journey to the mystical realm of Aetheria, where the Lumarians and Noctarians clash in a high-stakes battle for supremacy. In this top-down, real-time strategy game, you'll assume the role of a strategic commander, leading your chosen faction to victory by outwitting and overpowering your opponent.

Your primary objective is to destroy the enemy's base or camp while navigating a treacherous environment filled with challenges. Utilize your mastery of the environment's obstacles to your advantage, making sure they disrupt the enemy's plans and enhance your own.

Product Overview

Choose your allegiance to the Lumarians or the Noctarians, and engage in intense strategic warfare on the islands of Aetheria. This top-down real-time strategy game offers a unique and immersive experience where you control two different units, powered by advanced AI technology. These units automatically fire projectiles, allowing you to focus on tactical decision-making.

As a commander, you also have the extraordinary ability to construct a colossal weapon, the GIANT gun, on your island. This powerful weapon can be used to attack the enemy's base or camp, as well as to manipulate the environment in your favor.

But be prepared for the unpredictable forces of Aetheria's environment. It generates obstacles and unpredictable weather conditions that interact dynamically with the projectiles and the movement of your units and ships. Adapt and strategize as you navigate the challenges of this beautiful yet perilous archipelago setting.

The fate of Aetheria lies in your hands. Will you harness the energy of your island and conquer your foes, or will you succumb to the chaos of the Conflux? The battle for dominance begins now.
